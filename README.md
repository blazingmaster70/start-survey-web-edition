<h1>Start Survey (Web Version)</h1>

<p>
A fan-made browser remake of <strong>Start Survey</strong>, recreating the original
horror experience using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The survey begins with simple
questions, but as you continue answering <strong>YES</strong> and <strong>NO</strong>,
the atmosphere gradually becomes more unsettling. The goal is to preserve the
feeling of the original game while making it playable directly in a web browser.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>
    <li>Browser-based gameplay</li>
    <li>Progressive psychological horror</li>
    <li>Interactive YES/NO survey</li>
    <li>No installation required</li>
    <li>Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript</li>
    <li>Easy to modify and customize</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Play</h2>

<ol>
    <li>Open the website in your browser.</li>
    <li>Read each question carefully.</li>
    <li>Answer by clicking <strong>YES</strong> or <strong>NO</strong>.</li>
    <li>Continue until the survey reaches its ending.</li>
    <li>For the best experience, play in fullscreen with sound enabled.</li>
</ol>
